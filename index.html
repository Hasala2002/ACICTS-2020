Our Projects

Battle of the Maroons Live Score App

We developed this app to bring live scores of the Battle of the Maroons instantaneously to your mobile phone. So you can be updated about this heated event even though you are not in the stadium.

Live Streaming School Events

We have live streamed several events in the school such as the prize giving so that people who were unable to be present at the event could watch and experience the event to a certain extent.

Intraschool Competitions

BITS, the intraschool ICT competition series organized by the ACICTS has helped youngsters to discover their hidden talents and ultimately led them to achieve many victories.

About us

Through hard work and commitment ACICTS has become the most renowned ICT society in the country by gaining consecutive victories and titles in all the fields of ICT in both the Local and Global Arena. The Annual ICT event, organized by ACICTS is not only the most anticipated school-based ICT event, it has become one of the most iconic events belonging the whole ICT arena in Sri Lanka.
